# COMMERCIAL TERMS SUMMARY: IFM & PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

## 1. General Terms
- **Contract Duration:** 12 months (Renewable).
- **Grace Period:** 30 days notice for termination by either party.

## 2. Financial Terms
- **Mobilization Payment:** 15% of total annual contract value (Due on signing).
- **Annual/Quarterly/Monthly Payments:** As per the selected payment plan in the BIN App.
- **VAT:** All prices are subject to 5% UAE VAT.
- **Late Payment:** Service suspension after 15 days of non-payment.

## 3. Included Services
- **Preventive Maintenance (PPM):** Scheduled MEP, HVAC, and Lift servicing.
- **Reactive Maintenance:** Unlimited call-outs for MEP emergencies.
- **Digital Management:** Full access to Property Passport, Rent Ledger, and Owner Dashboard.
- **Tenant Management:** Digital onboarding and automated rent reminders.

## 4. Exclusions & Surcharges
- **Spare Parts:** Consumables and parts above AED 500 per instance are billed separately (subject to owner approval).
- **Capital Expenditure (CapEx):** Major system replacements (Chillers, Elevators, Pumps) are not included.
- **Emergency Surcharge:** No charge for SOS tickets within scope; non-contracted emergency work billed at AED 350/hour.

## 5. Cancellations & Renewals
- **Auto-Renewal:** Contracts auto-renew unless notified 60 days in advance.
- **Early Termination:** 25% of the remaining contract value payable if terminated without cause.
