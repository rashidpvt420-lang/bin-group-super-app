# BIN GROUP APP DEMO FLOW: THE SOVEREIGN WALKTHROUGH

## Phase 1: The Command Center (Admin View)
*Objective: Show the scale and professional oversight.*
1. **Admin Dashboard:** Show the 'Sovereign Control' panel. Highlight total properties under management and active maintenance missions.
2. **Technician Command:** Show the live GPS feed of technicians (if active) or the mission log.

## Phase 2: Asset Initialization (The Property Passport)
*Objective: Show the depth of data.*
1. **Property Passport Page:** Select a sample building. Show the 'Digital Twin' data: age, floors, lifts, and technical systems (HVAC, Fire Pump).
2. **Asset Health Index:** Explain how we track the technical 'pulse' of the building.

## Phase 3: Financial Transparency (The Rent Ledger)
*Objective: Solve the money pain.*
1. **Rent Ledger:** Show the automated balance tracking. Click on a 'Late' tenant to show the payment history.
2. **Bulk Tenant Import:** Demonstrate the CSV upload. Explain how 100+ tenants are invited via SMS/Email in seconds.

## Phase 4: Operations (Maintenance)
*Objective: Prove response speed.*
1. **SOS Feed:** Show the real-time emergency ticket log.
2. **Task Verification:** Show a completed maintenance ticket with 'Before/After' photos and GPS timestamps.

## Phase 5: The Owner's View (Owner Dashboard)
*Objective: Show what they get on their phone.*
1. **Owner App Overview:** Show the simplified ROI view, total rent collected, and active tickets.
2. **Reports:** Export a 'Portfolio Health Report' in one click.

## Phase 6: The Close (The Pricing Engine)
*Objective: Justify the investment.*
1. **Pricing Wizard:** Input the owner's tower details (Emirate, Zone, Units). Show the 2026 Matrix output live.
2. **Mobilization Breakdown:** Explain the 15% setup fee for the Digital Twin.
