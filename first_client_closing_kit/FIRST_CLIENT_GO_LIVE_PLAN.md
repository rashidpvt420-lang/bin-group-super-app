# FIRST CLIENT GO-LIVE PLAN: THE 7-DAY SPRINT

## Day 1: Authorization & Mobilization
- **Owner Action:** Sign Contract + Pay 15% Mobilization Fee.
- **BIN Action:** Assign a Dedicated Client Success Manager and Technical Audit Lead.

## Day 2: Document Collection
- **Owner Action:** Provide unit list, tenant list, and lease data (CSV/Excel).
- **BIN Action:** Kick off the **Data Scrubbing** process. Verify Emirates IDs and Lease dates against the ledger.

## Day 3: Property Passport Setup
- **BIN Action:** On-site technical audit. Tag all major equipment (Lifts, Pumps, HVAC) with digital QR codes.
- **System Action:** Initialize the building's **Digital Twin** in the BIN App.

## Day 4: Unit & Tenant Import
- **BIN Action:** Upload cleaned data via the Admin Control Center.
- **Verification:** Owner reviews the draft unit list for accuracy.

## Day 5: The Invitation Wave
- **System Action:** Trigger SMS/Email invitations to all 50+ tenants.
- **BIN Action:** Monitor open rates and assist tenants with app login.

## Day 6: Dashboard Review
- **BIN Action:** Live walkthrough of the **Owner Dashboard** with the real building data.
- **Verification:** Owner confirms visibility into the Rent Ledger and Active Missions.

## Day 7: Operational Go-Live
- **BIN Action:** 24/7 SOS dispatch is now active for all units. 
- **System Action:** First automated rent reminders are sent to tenants with upcoming payments.
