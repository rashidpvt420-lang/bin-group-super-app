# CLOSING OBJECTION HANDLING: 2026 PRICING & OPERATIONS

## 1. "Price is high."
"You're right, we aren't the cheapest in the market. But our pricing includes **preventive asset preservation** and a **live financial ledger**. Most companies quote low and then surprise you with hidden costs or allow your equipment to degrade. We protect your asset's valuation."

## 2. "We already have a maintenance company."
"Most owners do. But do they give you a live dashboard where you can see every repair photo-verified? We don't just provide tools; we provide **data sovereignty**. We can either manage your existing team through our platform or provide our own corps."

## 3. "Tenants may not use the app."
"Actually, tenants love the app because it gives them an SOS button and a clear record of their payments. We've seen a 40% improvement in tenant satisfaction scores in our pilot towers because communication is instant and digital."

## 4. "We don't have clean tenant data."
"That's exactly why you need us. Part of our 15% mobilization fee is for our data team to sit with your files, clean the records, and import them into the Property Passport. We fix the data gap for you."

## 5. "We only want maintenance, not property management."
"We offer Maintenance-Only contracts (AMC), but we still give you the Property Passport. You still need to know the health of your asset even if you handle the rent yourself. Our tech works both ways."

## 6. "We want to test first."
"We offer a 30-day 'Digital Twin Audit'. We set up the Property Passport and run one month of operations. If you aren't convinced by the visibility, you can cancel. But we charge a setup fee because the data work is intensive."

## 7. "We need legal review."
"Absolutely. Our contracts are standardized UAE commercial agreements. We've simplified the terms to 4 pages so your legal team can review them in 24 hours. Shall I send the draft over now?"

## 8. "We want cheaper monthly payments."
"If you pay annually, we remove the 6% facility fee. That's the most effective way to lower your costs while keeping the same Elite service level."
