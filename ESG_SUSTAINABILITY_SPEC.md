# BIN Group: ESG & Sustainability Framework

This document defines how BIN Group tracks and reports "Green Impact" to attract institutional investors and support the UAE's Net Zero 2050 goal.

---

## 💧 1. Water Conservation Tracking
When a water leak is repaired via the [IoT Protocol](./IOT_INTEGRATION_PROTOCOL.md), the system calculates:
*   **Formula**: `(Time to Fix) * (Avg Flow Rate) = Gallons Saved`.
*   **Metric**: Reported as "Litres of Water Saved" on the Owner Dashboard.

---

## ⚡ 2. Energy Optimization (AC Efficiency)
Since HVAC accounts for 70% of energy spend in the UAE, the system tracks:
*   **Preventive Impact**: Regular HVAC servicing improves efficiency by ~15%.
*   **Metric**: "Estimated AED Savings" based on improved asset performance vs. DEWA baseline rates.

---

## 📄 3. Paperless Digital Twin
*   **Impact**: 100% elimination of paper contracts, receipts, and work orders.
*   **Calculation**: `(Number of Contracts) * (8 pages) = Sheets of Paper Saved`.
*   **Metric**: "Trees Saved" counter in the Admin Global Hub.

---

## 🏦 4. Institutional Reporting (REIT Ready)
The system generates an **Annual ESG Certificate** for owners, which includes:
*   Total Portfolio CO2 reduction.
*   Total Water Waste prevented.
*   Percentage of vendors using eco-friendly materials (Green Marketplace).

---

## 📈 5. The "Green Score" Asset Ranking
Properties are tagged with an ESG score (1-100). Higher scores trigger:
*   **Lower Insurance Premiums**: Partnerships with UAE insurers for "Green-Managed" properties.
*   **Higher Resale Value**: Data-backed proof of a "High-Efficiency" asset.
