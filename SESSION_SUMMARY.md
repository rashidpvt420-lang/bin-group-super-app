## 💰 Business Logic Implemented

### ✅ Financial Waterfall
- Rent collected
- Deduct 5% BIN fee
- Deduct unpaid invoices
- Apply enterprise discount (3.3%)
- Transfer to owner (net payout)

### ✅ Two-Strike Suspension
- Trigger: 2+ unpaid invoices
- Effect: Block app, disable emergency services
- Recovery: Complete payment

### ✅ Enterprise Discount
- Trigger: 4+ properties
- Discount: 3.3% on quotes
- Applied: Automatically

### ✅ Health Score
- Base: 100 points
- -5 per open ticket
- +10 per completed PPM
- -5 for late payment
- +15 for high tenant rating
- +0 to +43 for response time

### ✅ Pricing (Hard-Coded)
- Studio AMC: AED 3,500/year
- Villa Small AMC: AED 12,000/year
- Villa Large AMC: AED 25,000/year
- Emergency: AED 350
- Turnover: AED 950-1,400
- Parts Markup: 20%
