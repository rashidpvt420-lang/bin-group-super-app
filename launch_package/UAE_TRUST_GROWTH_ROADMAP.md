# BIN GROUP UAE Trust Growth Roadmap

This roadmap converts the product audit into launchable trust systems for UAE maintenance and property management.

## Trust systems

- Property Trust Passport: health score, risk items, AC/water/electrical state, contract status, proof history, SLA, QR verification.
- Tenant Happiness Score: response time, completion time, tenant rating, dispute rate, repeat issue rate, emergency response history.
- AC Summer Protection: pre-summer inspection, filter schedule, gas leak check, compressor risk, emergency priority, tenant comfort score.
- Owner Approval Thresholds: small-work auto approval, mid-range notification, large-work approval, emergency audit, vendor rules.
- Move-in / Move-out Evidence: room photos, meters, AC, walls, appliances, keys/cards/remotes, tenant signature, owner report.
- Vendor Trust Marketplace: licence, insurance, category, job score, response time, complaint rate, warranty, suspension state.
- Warranty Ledger: warranty days, covered parts/labour, responsible technician/vendor, expiry, reopen path, repeat issue warning.
- SLA Credits: late emergency escalation, late technician warning, proof-required payout, repeat failure owner credit, dispute review.
- Broker Commission Lifecycle: referral link, owner conversion, contract signed, payment received, commission payable, withdrawal, admin approval.
- Launch Evidence Dashboard: Auth, Storage, Functions, FCM, GPS, AI, Payment, PWA, PDF, RTL, every-button, logout proof.

## UAE problems solved

- Tenant says nobody came: GPS check-in, timestamp, proof photo, status timeline.
- Owner asks why this cost: quote, RFQ, approval, invoice, before/after proof.
- Technician says job done but tenant disagrees: tenant approval/dispute and admin evidence review.
- WhatsApp messages get lost: intake converted to categorized ticket with property and unit context.
- Same AC/plumbing issue repeats: warranty ledger and repeat-issue detection.
- Broker commission is forgotten: referral and commission lifecycle.
- Owner cannot see ROI: maintenance trend, asset health, avoided emergency cost, tenant retention signal.
- Staff and HR records are messy: digital staff dossier, payslip support, HR letters, safety readiness.

## Launch rule

Controlled public pilot can proceed with selected properties. Full unrestricted public launch should remain blocked until required production proof gates are passed or formally waived.
