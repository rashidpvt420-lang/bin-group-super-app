# BIN GROUP COMMERCIAL PRICING MODEL: 2026 STRATEGIC MASTER MATRIX

1.Executive Summary

The 2026 Pricing Matrix is the sovereign benchmark for property management and maintenance in the UAE. It transitions BIN GROUP from flat-fee pricing to a risk-adjusted, zone-based, and asset-specific institutional model.

2.The Zone Framework

Pricing is adjusted based on the operational complexity and service standard of the asset location:

Zone A (Premium): Multiplier 1.3x. Luxury, waterfront (Marina, Palm), branded residences, high-SLA assets.

Zone B (Standard): Multiplier 1.0x. Mid-market, high-density, mixed-use corridors (JVC, Business Bay).

Zone C (Budget/Industrial): Multiplier 0.75x. Industrial zones, labor accommodation, logistics hubs.

3.Emirate Multipliers

Standardized adjustments for regional operational costs:

Dubai: 1.15

Abu Dhabi: 1.10

Sharjah: 0.90

RAK / Ajman / Fujairah: 0.80 - 0.88
