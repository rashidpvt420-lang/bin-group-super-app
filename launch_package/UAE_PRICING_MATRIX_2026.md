# STRATEGIC MASTER PRICING MATRIX: 2026 BENCHMARKS (UAE)

## 1. Executive Summary

The 2026 UAE Pricing Matrix is designed to provide property owners and managers with a standardized, transparent, and data-driven framework for Maintenance (AMC), Property Management (PM), and Integrated Facilities Management (IFM) contracts.

## 2. The Zone Framework

Assets are categorized into three zones based on location premium and operational complexity:

| Zone | Label | Multiplier | Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zone A** | Premium | 1.30 | Palm Jumeirah, Downtown, Dubai Marina, Saadiyat |
| **Zone B** | Standard | 1.00 | JVC, Al Barsha, Business Bay, Reem Island |
| **Zone C** | Industrial | 0.75 | Jebel Ali Free Zone, DIP, Musaffah |

## 3. Emirate Cost Adjustments

- **Dubai:** +15% (High regulatory & labor costs)
- **Abu Dhabi:** +10% (Compliance & safety standards)
- **Sharjah:** -10% (Operational density)
- **Northern Emirates:** -15% to -20%

## 4. Detailed Asset Class Matrix (25 Categories)

### A. Residential

- **Standard Apartment:** Benchmarked for high-density buildings.
- **Luxury Apartment:** Includes premium fit-outs and higher SLA requirements.
- **Standard Villa:** Focus on MEP, AC, and basic landscape.
- **Luxury Estate Villa:** Includes pool, advanced automation, and large grounds.
- **Short-Term Apartment:** High-frequency maintenance and cleaning requirements.
- **Short-Term Villa:** Intensive management and seasonal load handling.

### B. Commercial & Retail

- **Small Office:** Efficient, unit-based maintenance.
- **Commercial Tower:** Scaled sqft pricing with high technical complexity.
- **Retail Mall:** High-traffic mechanical, plumbing, and safety requirements.
- **Retail Showroom:** Large floor plates with specific electrical needs.

### C. Industrial & Logistics

- **General Warehouse:** Base-level maintenance for storage.
- **Cold Storage:** Critical cooling system maintenance (+20% premium).
- **Logistics Hub:** Heavy machinery and high-duty pavement management.
- **Labor Camp:** Per-bed pricing for high-occupancy environments.

### D. Hospitality & Majlis

- **Mid-Scale Hotel:** Balanced guest-facing and back-of-house maintenance.
- **Luxury Resort:** High-SLA grounds, pools, and specialized MEP.
- **Private Majlis:** Low frequency but extremely high standard requirement.
- **Government Majlis:** High-compliance, 24/7 availability requirement.

### E. Specialized

- **Primary Clinic:** Basic healthcare compliance (SIRA/DHA).
- **Large Hospital:** Critical systems (Medical Gas, Power Redundancy).
- **School:** Seasonal maintenance and child-safety compliance.
- **University:** Large-scale campus FM and technical labs.
- **Sports Stadium:** High-load seasonal events and lighting.
- **Data Center:** Ultra-critical power and cooling (Tier III/IV standards).
- **Mixed-Use Development:** Complex multi-stakeholder aggregation.

## 5. Contract Models

- **Maintenance (AMC):** Preventive and corrective maintenance only.
- **Property Management (PM):** Tenant onboarding, leasing, and ledger management.
- **Integrated FM (IFM):** Full-service "hands-off" ownership.

| Factor | Condition | Premium |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Height** | 15+ Floors | +8% |
| **Extreme Height** | 40+ Floors | +15% |
| **Lifts** | > 10 Units | +10% |

| **BMU/Cradle** | High-Rise Access | +6% |

## 7. Compliance & Risk

- **DCD / Civil Defense:** Mandatory systems maintenance included.
- **SIRA CCTV:** Critical security audit compliance.
- **ADCD / Abu Dhabi:** Specific safety standards (+10% premium).
