## Tracking Definitions
- **Cold:** Lead identified but no contact made.
- **Contacted:** Initial message sent (WhatsApp/Email).
- **Interested:** Replied positively or requested info.
- **Demo Scheduled:** Time set for live walkthrough.
- **Proposal Sent:** 2026 Matrix quote delivered.
- **Negotiation:** Reviewing terms/discount/pilot scope.
- **Closed Won:** Contract signed + Mobilization paid.
- **Closed Lost:** Lead declined (document reason in 'Last Action').
