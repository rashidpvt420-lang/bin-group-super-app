# PILOT SCOPE: 52-UNIT RESIDENTIAL TOWER (ABU DHABI)

## 1. Primary Objective
To demonstrate the operational efficiency of the **BIN GROUP Super App** in managing a mid-size residential asset, focusing on maintenance response and rent ledger accuracy.

## 2. Commercial Framework
- **Annual Contract Value:** AED 75,344 (Institutional Matrix 2026).
- **Mobilization Fee:** 15% (AED 11,301) payable on Day 1.
- **Payment Plan:** Quarterly Installments (AED 18,836).
- **SLA Tier:** Premium (2hr Emergency Response).

## 3. The BIN GROUP "Visibility Guarantee"
- If the **Owner Dashboard** is not live with real building data within 7 business days of mobilization, BIN GROUP will credit 5% of the annual contract back to the owner.

## 4. BIN GROUP Responsibilities
- 24/7 MEP Reactive Maintenance for all 52 units.
- Monthly Preventive Maintenance (PPM) for Lifts, HVAC, and Fire Pumps.
- Digital Onboarding of all 52 tenants.
- Monthly Automated Rent Collection Reporting.

## 5. Owner Responsibilities
- Provide accurate Tenant CSV data (Names, Phones, Emails).
- Provide copy of Title Deed and Unit Layouts.
- Grant site access for QR tagging of technical assets.
- Approve spare parts above AED 500 within 4 business hours.

## 6. Exclusions
- Major capital replacement (CapEx).
- Structural / Civil engineering work.
- External landscaping beyond the immediate perimeter.
