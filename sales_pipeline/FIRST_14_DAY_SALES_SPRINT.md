# THE 14-DAY SALES SPRINT: BIN GROUP FIRST CLIENT ACQUISITION

## Week 1: Lead Gen & Outreach
- **Day 1: Asset Preparation.** Finalize demo login for Admin and Owner apps. Ensure 'Sample Tower' data is flawless.
- **Day 2: The List of 20.** Identify 20 owners/managers using the target list template. Priority on mid-size residential towers.
- **Day 3: The WhatsApp Wave.** Send the first batch of 20 WhatsApp introductions. Goal: 5 replies.
- **Day 4: Phone Follow-up.** Call everyone who didn't reply to WhatsApp. "Did you see the dashboard video?"
- **Day 5: Proposal Initial Batch.** Send preliminary 'Capability Proposals' to the top 5 interested leads.
- **Day 6: Demo Booking.** Follow up to schedule 15-minute live walkthroughs.
- **Day 7: The Demo Marathon.** Conduct at least 3 live demos. Focus on the 'Rent Ledger' and 'Property Passport'.

## Week 2: Closing & Onboarding
- **Day 8: Proposal Revision.** Customize pricing based on the owner's actual unit count and asset complexity.
- **Day 9: Second Follow-up.** Send 'Success Story' snippets from the 102-tenant pilot to build trust.
- **Day 10: Terms Closing.** Negotiate the Pilot Guarantee (30-day trial). Get verbal approval.
- **Day 11: Document Collection.** Request Trade License, Title Deed, and Tenant CSV.
- **Day 12: Data Audit.** Scrub the owner's tenant data. Clean duplicates and verify lease dates.
- **Day 13: System Initialization.** Import units and tenants into the production app. Setup Property Passport.
- **Day 14: Final Confirmation.** Walk the owner through their live dashboard with their real data. Kick off Go-Live.
