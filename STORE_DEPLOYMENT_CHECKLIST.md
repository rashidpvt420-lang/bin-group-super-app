# BIN Group: App Store Deployment & Compliance (UAE)

Specific requirements for successfully launching the BIN Group mobile applications on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store in the Middle East.

---

## 🏗️ 1. App Store Categorization

* **Primary Category**: Finance (Property Management/FinTech).
* **Secondary Category**: Lifestyle (Home Services).
* **Minimum Audience Age**: 18+ (due to legal contracts and payment handling).

---

## 🔒 2. Privacy Manifest & Labels

As per UAE PDPL and store policies, the following data labels must be declared:

| Data Type | Usage | Linked to Identity? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Location** | Exact tracking for Tech Dispatch. | Yes. |
| **Financial Info** | Rent facilitation via Payment Gateway. | Yes. |
| **Identifiers** | Emirates ID / UAE PASS UUID. | Yes. |
| **Contact Info** | WhatsApp/SMS notifications. | Yes. |

---

## 🇦🇪 3. Local Store Optimization (LSO)

* **Keywords**: "PropTech UAE", "Ejari Dubai", "Rent Payment", "Maintenance Dubai", "Estate Management".
* **Screenshots**: Must feature the **Arabic RTL UI** in at least 50% of the preview slides to show local commitment.
* **Privacy Policy Link**: Direct link to the [Data & Compliance Plan](./DATA_COMPLIANCE_PLAN.md).

---

## 🛠️ 4. Technical Submission Checklist

* [ ] **UAE Region Only**: Restrict initial release to the UAE App Store to prevent global support overhead.

* [ ] **EID Encryption Proof**: Documentation ready for Apple/Google regarding the encryption of scanned identity documents.
* [ ] **Support Numbers**: Dedicated UAE toll-free or WhatsApp business number listed.
* [ ] **RTL Consistency**: Verify that the App Store Description is available in both English and Proper Arabic.
