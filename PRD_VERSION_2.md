## 🧠 2. Feature Modules

### A. Intelligence & Analytics Engine
1.  **Property Performance Dashboard**:
    *   **Spec**: Real-time aggregation of income, expenses, vacancy % and ROI.
    *   **User Story**: As an owner, I want to see my portfolio's financial health to make exit/hold decisions.
    *   **Acceptance**: PDF exportable reports, multi-timeframe filters, and community-level benchmarking.
2.  **Predictive Maintenance (AI Alerts)**:
    *   **Spec**: Machine learning analysis of building age, maintenance logs, and IoT sensor streams.
    *   **Acceptance**: Urgency-scored alert list; push notifications for high-risk assets (HVAC/Elevators).

### B. Tenant Super Portal
1.  **Lifestyle Dashboard**:
    *   **Spec**: Unified hub for Rent payments, Lease viewing, and maintenance tracking.
    *   **Acceptance**: Integrated payment gateway, digital move-in/out checklists, and secure document vault.
2.  **Multilingual Gemini Concierge**:
    *   **Spec**: 24/7 AI conversational support in English and Arabic.
    *   **Story**: As a tenant, I want instant answers to lease term queries without calling management.

### C. Advanced Technical Operations & PM
1.  **AI Delegated Dispatch**:
    *   **Spec**: Auto-assignment of tickets based on technician skill-ratings, proximity, and urgency.
    *   **Acceptance**: Logic for auto-escalation if SLA thresholds are breached.
2.  **Preventive Maintenance Scheduler**:
    *   **Spec**: Automated recurring tasks for HVAC (pre-summer), fire safety, and lifts.
    *   **Acceptance**: Visual calendar for owners/admins; automated work-order generation 48h before task.

### D. Financial & Compliance Engine
1.  **Automated Rent & Cheque Ledger**:
    *   **Spec**: Digital tracking of PDCs with scan-to-track OCR and bank status sync.
    *   **Acceptance**: Workflow for bounced cheque alerts and automated late-fee calculation.
2.  **Regulatory GovBridge**:
    *   **Spec**: Automated Ejari registration and DEWA/Utility clearance tracking.

### E. Field Intelligence & Market Alpha
1.  **Vacancy & Occupancy Heatmaps**:
    *   **Spec**: Real-time visual dashboard of unit availability and lease expiry alerts.
    *   **Acceptance**: Automated renewal suggestions triggered 90 days before expiry.
2.  **Mobile Inspection & Checklist Hub**:
    *   **Spec**: Standardized walk-through modules for pre/post-maintenance verification.
    *   **Acceptance**: Mandatory timestamped photo/video evidence per checklist item.
