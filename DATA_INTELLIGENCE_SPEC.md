# BIN Group: Data Intelligence & KPI Spec

This document defines the advanced metrics required for growth-stage reporting and institutional investor auditing.

---

## 📈 1. Operational Efficiency (The "Internal" Health)

| KPI | Formula | Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **First-Visit Fix (FVF)** | (Jobs fixed in 1 visit) / (Total Jobs) | > 85% |
| **SLA Adherence** | (On-time arrivals) / (Total Dispatches) | > 92% |
| **Vendor NPS** | Weighted average of Tenant ratings post-job. | > 4.5 Stars |

---

## 💰 2. Asset Performance (The "Owner" Wealth)

| KPI | Formula | Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Net Yield (ROI)** | (Annual Rent - OpEx) / (Asset Value) | > 7% |
| **Maintenance Intensity** | (Total Maintenance Cost) / (Gross Rent) | < 12% |
| **Vacancy Cost** | (Days Vacant) * (Daily Market Rent) | < AED 5,000/yr |

---

## 🚀 3. Growth & SaaS Metrics (The "Empire" Scale)

* **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: The total management fee revenue generated by an owner over a 10-year period.
* **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Total marketing spend / Number of new properties onboarded.
* **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Target > 3:1 for sustainable scaling.
* **Expansion Rate**: Percentage of owners who add a second property within 18 months.

---

## 🧠 4. AI-Driven Predictive KPIs

* **Churn Risk Score**: Predictive score (0-1) for tenants based on maintenance complaints and late payments.
* **Asset Health Index**: Combined score of AC age, plumbing history, and structural integrity (triggers proactive replacement vs. repair).
