# PRIVACY POLICY — BIN GROUP

**Effective Date**: March 13, 2026  
**Company**: BIN GROUP – General Maintenance & Property Management LLC

---

## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how BIN GROUP (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, retains, and protects personal information through our mobile apps, web portals, and services (the “Platform”). This policy is drafted in accordance with the **UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021)** and applicable regional regulations.

## 2. Data Controller
**Company**: BIN GROUP – General Maintenance & Property Management LLC  
**Contact Email**: privacy@bingroup.ae  
**DPO Contact**: dpo@bingroup.ae

## 3. Information We Collect

### A. User-Provided Data
*   **Identity & Contact**: Legal name, phone number, email address, and Emirates ID (for verification via UAE PASS).
*   **Property Data**: Address, unit details, and title deed metadata.
*   **Operational Media**: Photos and videos uploaded for maintenance triage and proof of work.
*   **Financial Data**: IBAN or payout details (processed by secure payment partners).

### B. Automatically Collected Data
*   **Usage Info**: Device identifiers, IP addresses, and app interaction logs.
*   **Location**: Geolocation data (for technician dispatch and live tracking) with user consent.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data under the following legal grounds:
*   **Contractual Necessity**: To fulfill maintenance requests, manage lease renewals, and transfer rent payments.
*   **Explicit Consent**: For marketing notifications and specific media processing.
*   **Legal Obligation**: To comply with UAE tax, audit, and anti-money laundering laws.

## 5. How We Use Your Information
*   To manage user accounts and provide "God-Mode" and "Owner/Tenant" portal access.
*   To conduct AI-driven maintenance triage and technician dispatch.
*   To facilitate secure "Rent Now, Pay Later" (RNPL) transactions.
*   To send critical SOS alerts and service notifications.
*   To improve platform performance and security.

## 6. Sharing & Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:
*   **Authorized Contractors**: Technicians assigned to your work orders.
*   **Service Providers**: Secure cloud hosts and payment gateways (Stripe/UAEDDS).
*   **Regulatory Bodies**: UAE government authorities where required by law.

## 7. Data Residency & Transfers
Your personal data is hosted in secure regions compliant with UAE standards. Any cross-border transfers are conducted under strict contractual safeguards to ensure PDPL-level protection.

## 8. Retention
Data is retained for the duration of your user experience and for a minimum of 7 years where required for UAE financial and technical audit compliance.

## 9. Your Rights
Under the UAE PDPL, you have the right to request:
*   Access to your data.
*   Correction of inaccurate information.
*   Deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
*   Withdrawal of consent.
To exercise these rights, contact **dpo@bingroup.ae**.

## 10. Security
We implement SSL/TLS encryption, role-based access control (RBAC), and encryption at rest to safeguard your data.
