# BIN GROUP Company Profile 60-Second Concept QA

## Goal

Make the public company profile simple enough for owners, tenants, technicians, and brokers to understand in under one minute.

## Public message

No calls. No confusion. Every property job tracked and proven.

## Public users

- Owner: add property, choose service, approve payment, track proof and cost.
- Tenant: report issue, add photo, follow status, approve or dispute after proof.
- Technician: accept job, update status, upload before and after proof, close correctly.
- Broker: submit lead, track approval, follow commission after verified deal.

## Trust proof

The profile now highlights licensed UAE company identity, clear service flow, photos, location context, time-stamped records, tenant approval or dispute, owner cost visibility, and English/Arabic support.

## Internal wording rule

The public company profile should stay focused on public users and should not use internal control-role wording.
