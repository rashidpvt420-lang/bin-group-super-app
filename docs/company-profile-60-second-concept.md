# Company Profile 60-Second Concept Update

This update simplifies the public company profile so owners, tenants, technicians, and brokers understand BIN GROUP in under one minute.

## Public concept

No calls. No confusion. Every property job tracked and proven.

## Four journeys

- Owner: add property, choose service, approve payment, track cost, work, reports, and proof.
- Tenant: report issue, add photo, follow status, approve or dispute after proof.
- Technician: accept job, go on site, update status, upload before/after proof, close correctly.
- Broker: submit lead, track approval, follow commission after verified deal.

## Trust bar

Licensed UAE company, clear service flow, photos, GPS/location context, time-stamped records, tenant approval or dispute, owner cost visibility, and English/Arabic support.

## Rule

The public company profile should not mention internal control roles. The page should explain the concept simply for public users.
