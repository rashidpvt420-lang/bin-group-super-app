# BIN Group: Growth & Loyalty Matrix (UAE Expansion)

This matrix defines the B2B and B2C "Growth Triggers" to scale the platform and reduce user churn.

---

## 🏗️ 1. Owner Acquisition (B2B Growth)

| Program | Trigger | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Portfolio Expansion** | Adding a 2nd+ property. | 0% Management fee for first 90 days of new unit. |
| **Asset Manager Referral**| Referral of a fellow landlord. | AED 1,000 credit toward future maintenance work. |
| **Multi-Year Commitment**| Pre-signing for 3+ years. | Exclusive access to "Predictive Failure" IoT sensors at zero cost. |

---

## 🏠 2. Tenant Retention (B2C Loyalty)

| Program | Trigger | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Renewal** | Signing renewal 60 days before expiry. | Complimentary "Deep Cleaning" or "AC Sterilization" service. |
| **On-Time Payment** | 12 consecutive months of on-time rent. | Boosted "Tenant Score" leading to lower security deposit at next property. |
| **Perfect Handover** | Move-out with zero damage detected by [AI Checklist](./PRD_VERSION_2.md).| Instant full security deposit release + "Bin Preferred" status. |

---

## 📈 3. Marketplace Network Effects
*   **Vendor Loyalty**: Vendors who maintain a >4.8 star rating for 6 months get "Premium Placement" in the [Marketplace](./VENDOR_ONBOARDING_WORKFLOW.md).
*   **Community Cross-Sell**: Tenants can use "BIN Points" (earned from on-time payments) to book painting, moving, or logistics services at a 10% discount.

---

## 🚀 4. Virality Framework (The Invite Loop)
1.  **Shared Dashboards**: Owners can invite "Co-Owners" or "Spouses" to view ROI, acting as a natural referral for the brand.
2.  **QR Sharing**: Technicians can give "Referral Stickers" to building security/neighbors, earning a bonus for every signed lead.
