## 📈 Monitoring Definitions

### 1. Throughput Metrics
- **Onboarding Submissions**: Total property nodes submitted by Owners.
- **Payment Verifications**: Count of confirmed 15% mobilization deposits.
- **Quote Approvals**: Total technical estimates authorized by Owners.

### 2. Efficiency Metrics
- **Tickets Dispatched**: Count of missions successfully locked to a Specialist.
- **SLA Breach Events**: Instances where the 4-hour or 24-hour response thresholds were exceeded.

### 3. Stability & Integrity Metrics
- **Orphan/Conflict Records**: Count of records detected without verified relational anchors.
- **Push Delivery Failures**: Logged FCM rejections.
- **Move-out/Reassignment**: Total asset nodes vacated and re-provisioned.
